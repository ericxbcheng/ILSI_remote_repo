Geometry of the Contamination Event

In this section, the user must determine the affected shape of the contamination event. Depending on the size of the area affected, these events might be: Point Source or Area based. To provide a better insight of how these events might look like, the following scenarios are provided:

Point Source Contamination Scenarios:

  • Fecal material found in between the rows or beds due to animal intrusion
  • Fecal material found in between the rows or beds due to presence of birds in the area
  • Visible damage to the crop due to animal intrusion (for self-feeding)

Area Based contamination Scenarios: *

  • Irrigation of the fields with contaminated water
  • “Irrigation” of the fields with rainfall until field is flooded
  • Fertilization of fields using unproperly composted manure
  • Presence or proximity of cattle ranching to the vegetable farm
